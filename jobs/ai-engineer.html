AI Engineer

Role

Title: Product Engineer

Location: London, UK

Salary range: £100,000-£150,000 p.a. + equity, depending on experience (up to £170,000 for candidates with exceptional relevant experience)

Apply by: Email us at work@writewithmarker.com and tell us a little bit about yourself, along with your CV or a link to your CV site.

What is Marker?

Marker is an AI-native Word Processor. Join us in building the next generation user interface for serious writers, enhanced by collaborative workflows and augmented by human-centred agentic AI editors.

We are a small, ambitious company using cutting edge technology to make writing a little bit easier.

What you’ll do at Marker

  • Build web user interfaces using React/Next.js and TypeScript
  • Contribute to our real-time collaborative backend (Cloudflare Workers, TypeScript, CRDTs, PostgreSQL)
  • Define, architect, implement and deliver features end to end, from conception to production
  • Be a part of the early founding team to guide the future of our products and company at large

What we can offer

  • A calm, human-friendly work environment among kind and experienced professionals
  • Fun, creative, novel, and interesting technical work (if you’re into that sort of thing)
  • An opportunity to work with and learn about the latest advancements in AI engineering and language models
  • As much responsibility and growth opportunities as you want to take on

Are you a good fit for this role?

In order to be successful in this role, you will recognise yourself in the following:

  • You have at least a few years of experience building products for the web using React
  • You’re not afraid contributing to backend codebases (see our stack below)
  • You’re familiar with some of the other technologies in Our Stack (see below)
  • You have a user-focused product mindset and good design sensibilities
  • You are interested in learning about generative AI technologies
  • You are interested in building innovative user interfaces requiring creativity, problem-solving and research

An exceptional candidate for this role would be able to demonstrate some of the following:

  • Experience with building advanced Rich Text Editing interfaces
  • Experience with collaborative real-time technologies (CRDTs, Y.js, sync engines, etc.)
  • Experience building production-grade local-first or offline-first applications
  • Experience using LLMs in production, especially multi-agentic systems
  • Interest in writing (fiction, non-fiction, essays)

However, you are NOT expected to:

  • Be an expert in AI, NLP, or Machine Learning
  • Be a designer — you’ll be working together with our CPO and designer Ryan Bowman
  • Be experienced with every library and product in our stack from day one — you’ll work closely with our engineering lead Jani Eväkallio to learn the ropes
  • Have a degree in Computer Science — we value practical experience over credentials

Our stack

You’ll be working with the following technologies:

  • Frontend: React, TypeScript, Tailwind
  • Text Editor: Tiptap, ProseMirror
  • Backend: Cloudflare Workers, PostgreSQL
  • Realtime Sync: CRDTs with Y.js
  • Future plans: Electron and other technologies to be determined together

You’ll also have the opportunity to learn and contribute to the backend technologies that power the AI magic behind Marker.

Our AI engine uses a cutting edge agentic approach with models ranging from open source fine-tuned systems to the latest reasoning models from Anthropic and OpenAI.
